🧩 Tips & Tricks to Win

Want to dominate the court like a pro? Follow these expert tips:

  1. Keep Moving! Don’t stand still — motion creates scoring chances.

  2. Jump Earlier: For dunks or layups, time your jump just before the basket.

  3. Use the Backboard: For mid-range shots, bounce the ball off the backboard for a higher chance to score.

  4. Defend Smartly: Anticipate your opponent’s moves — block shots, don’t chase.
  5. Watch the Timer: Manage your time well and look for quick plays near the end.

  6. Pass Before Shooting: Create open shots by passing to teammates in better positions.

  7. Stay Cool: Missing shots is part of the game — focus on rebounds and defense.
